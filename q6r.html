"When Poe was originally buried in 1849, he was placed in an unmarked grave. Over the years, the site became overgrown with weeds. Eventually, George W. Spence (the Sexton), placed there a small block of sandstone, bearing a carved number “80” (Phillips, Poe the Man, p.1512). Reports of Poe’s anonymous and unkempt grave began to circulate, first privately then in the newspapers." (https://www.eapoe.org/balt/poegrave.htm)
